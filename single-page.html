Projects

Maps are a balance of art and science, which I strive to achieve in the cartographic and data elements of my maps. I believe the best maps are minimalist in nature, using a refined color palette and embracing small details like transparency. I like when the final cartographic product looks simple, yet elegant, but really took quite a bit of analysis and finesse to make.
